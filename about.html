Our success and company history

Welcome to Sofia Plastic

Established in 1990, Sofia Plastic has spearheaded the hanger manufacturing industry, consistently raising the bar with our unwavering dedication to excellence and innovation. Our journey over the years has been marked by a steadfast commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer-centric values, solidifying our position as the preferred choice for businesses in need of reliable hanger solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision at Sofia Plastic is to redefine industry standards, setting the bar for excellence in hanger manufacturing worldwide. We aim to be the premier innovators and suppliers of top-tier hanger solutions, recognized for our uncompromising commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sofia Plastic is to craft premium-quality plastic hangers that surpass expectations. We are dedicated to sustainability, constantly exploring eco-conscious materials and manufacturing practices to minimize our environmental footprint. Through relentless innovation, impeccable craftsmanship, and unparalleled customer support, we are committed to becoming the foremost choice for individuals and businesses alike, revolutionizing the way people organize, showcase, and cherish their clothing.
